What's Next For BoxIT Services?

BoxIT Services has had success leading struggling businesses, like those in the contractor industry, towards financial stability and growth. Now we are looking to spread our expertise with other contractors via a campaign of kiosk installation.

Ten kiosk/end caps will be installed in Home Depots in the greater Washington area and will serve as our flagship advertising effort for the Filter Program we established during our partnership with a contractor client. By focusing on this beneficial plan we believe we can modernize HVAC service and bring the BoxIT brand to more people.

Filter Program

Partnered with a Washington HVAC contractor company, BoxIT Services developed the Filter Program. By subscribing to the Filter Program, the customer gains monthly service and the peace of mind not having to stay on top of their HVAC’s maintenance. Consistent air filter replacement guarantees high air quality and temperature control.

Passing on the responsibility of HVAC monitoring via the Filter Program is the best way to guarantee these benefits and generate clients for participating contractors.
